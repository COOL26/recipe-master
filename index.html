Classic Cheesecake Recipe

drag_indicator

look no further for a creamy and ultra smooth classic Cheesecake recipe! paired with a buttery graham cracker crust, no one can it's simple decadence. For the best results bake in a water bath.

Ingredients

    Graham Cracker Crust

  1. 1 and 1/2 cups (150g) graham cracker crumbs(about 10 full graham crackers)
  2. 5 Tablespoons (70g) unsalted butter, melted
  3. 1/4 cup (50g) granulated sugar

    Cheesecake

  1. four 8-ounces blocks (904g) full-fat cream cheese, softened to room temperature
  2. 1 cup (200g) granulated sugar
  3. 1 cup (204g) full-fat sour cream, at room temperature
  4. 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  5. 2 teaspoons fresh lemon juice (optional, but recommended)
  6. 3 large eggs, at room temperature
  7. topping suggestions: salted caramel, lemon curd, strawberry topping, chocolate ganache, red wine chocolate ganache,fresh fruit, whipped cream, or raspberry sause (recipe in notes)

Instructions

  1. 1
    Starting off: Adjust the oven rack to the lower-middle position and preheat oven to 350°F(177°C)
  2. 2
    Make the crust: Using a food processor, pulse the graham crackers into the crumbs. Pour into a medium bowl and stir in sugar and melted butter until combined. (You can also pulse it all together in the food processor.) Mixture will be sandy. Press firmly into the bottom and slightly up the sides of a 9-inch or 10-inch springform pan. No need to grease the pan first. I use the bottom of a measuring cup to pack the crust down tightly. Pre-bake for 8 minutes. Remove from the oven and place the hot pan on a large piece of aluminum foil. The foil will wrap around the pan for the water bath in step 4. Allow crust to slightly cool as you prepare the filling.
